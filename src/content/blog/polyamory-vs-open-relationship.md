**In short:** An open relationship is usually romantically exclusive but sexually open – a couple remains the emotional core. Polyamory allows multiple simultaneous *romantic* relationships, often on equal footing. Both belong to the spectrum of ethical non-monogamy (ENM).

## The Open Relationship

In a classic open relationship there is a primary couple that permits sexual contact outside the partnership – emotional bonds with others are usually not the goal. Agreements typically revolve around safer-sex rules, transparency, and what should be shared.

## Polyamory

Polyamory literally means "many loves". Here, multiple simultaneous, emotionally significant relationships are explicitly welcomed. Structures range from hierarchical (with one primary partnership) to egalitarian networks where all connections carry equal weight.
